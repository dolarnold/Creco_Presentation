📱 How CRECO App Works

Kenya's Smart Fact-Checking Platform - Turning Every Citizen into a Truth Guardian

1
🤔📱
Citizen Alert

🚨 You See Something That Doesn't Look Right

You're scrolling through WhatsApp, Facebook, or watching news and come across information that makes you wonder: "Is this actually true?" Maybe it's about elections, COVID-19, government policies, or anything affecting Kenya.

Example: "I heard polling stations are closing early" or "New COVID restrictions starting tomorrow" - but you're not sure if it's real
⬇️
2
📸✨
Quick Report

📲 Report It in Seconds

Open the CRECO app on your phone, take a screenshot or type the suspicious claim, add where you saw it (optional), and hit send. The whole process takes less than 30 seconds. You can even report anonymously if you prefer!

Just like taking a photo and sending it to a friend - but you're sending it to professional fact-checkers instead
⬇️
3
🤖⚡
AI Assistant

🧠 Smart System Organizes Everything

Behind the scenes, our intelligent system reads your submission and automatically sorts it. It knows if it's about health, politics, or breaking news. It also checks if someone else has already reported the same thing and decides how urgent it is.

Think of it as a super-smart receptionist who instantly knows which doctor you need to see and how urgent your case is
⬇️
4
🕵️‍♂️📋
Professional Check

🔍 CRECO Experts Investigate Like Detectives

Professional fact-checkers receive your claim on their organized dashboard. They contact original sources, check with government offices, interview experts, and follow strict journalism standards to determine what's true or false.

Like professional detectives: they don't guess - they find evidence, call witnesses, and verify facts before giving you an answer
⬇️
5
🏛️💾
Truth Database

📚 Everything Saved in Digital Truth Library

All verified information is safely stored in a digital library that works like Kenya's National Archives - but for facts! This creates a permanent, searchable database of verified information that grows stronger over time.

Future Kenyans can search "What did CRECO verify about 2025 elections?" and find all the facts in one place
⬇️
6
✅📢
Truth Delivered

🎯 Get Clear, Trustworthy Answers

You receive a notification with a clear verdict: TRUE ✅, FALSE ❌, PARTIALLY TRUE ⚠️, or NEEDS MORE INFO 🔄. Each answer includes simple explanations, shows the evidence, and is written in plain language anyone can understand.

"VERDICT: FALSE - Polling stations close at 5 PM nationwide, not 3 PM. Source: Official IEBC statement dated [date]"

🇰🇪 The Result: A More Informed Kenya

When citizens, technology, and professional fact-checkers work together, truth wins over rumors and Kenya makes better decisions based on facts, not fiction.
